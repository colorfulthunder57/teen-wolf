SEASON RUNDOWNS!!!

  • Season 1

    • After being convinced by his friend, Stiles Stilinski, to search for a dead body the night before the first day of school, Scott Mcall is attacked in the woods by an unkown creature. Subsequently, He experinces heightened smeel, sight, and...anger. While Stiles tries to convonce him he is a werewolf, Scott is chasing a mysterious new girl, Allison Argent(little does he know she comes from a long line of hunters). Throughout season 2 Scott struggles finding his place in the world, girl troubles, and finding out who bit him. Peter Hale, a previously catatonic alpha, goes on a killing spree in an attempt to get Scott to join the pack. Scott also struggles with staying alive since Allison's father is on a mission to destroy any and all traces of supernatural beings in Beacon Hills.

  • Season 2

    • The show starts with Lydia's vanishing and a spree of murders. Lydia Martin struggles with coping after being bitten by Peter Hale and her growing new powers as a banchee. Isaac, a new beta, is arrested for the murder of his father, despite his innocence. Derek is willing to go to any means neccessary in order to get Scott in his pack. Midway through the season they discover the mysterioius creature's name... a kanima( a slithering reptilian creature that can paralyze with its tail). They discover a kid from there school is controlling the creature, and using its abilities to kill the 1985 Beacon Hills Swim team(the kids almost drowned him at a party when he was a kid). Allison's grandfather, kills the kid in an attempt to win the Kanimas powers.

  • Season 3

    • Kira, a kitsune from Ohio, moves to California with her family only to be met with the aggressive packs of Beacon Hills. Previously, Allison and Scott broke up(her father threatened to kill Scott), so Scott is looking for a new love interest. An alpha pack has come to beacon hills to challenge Derek and win over Scott. A dark mysterious nogitsune, Void, has re-emerged and is spreading chaos. In an attempt to save Kira from a crazy hunter, Stiles is electrocuted and is possessed by Void. The whole season centers around trying to stop Void, and more importantly, saving Stiles.

  • Season 4

    • Two months after the end of season 3 everyone is trying to heal from the losses of the final battle of season 3. Beacon hills dosen't leave them much time for recovery. Kate Argent, a hunter that killed Derek;s family, has been reborn as a were-jaguar. Along with Kate causing trouble, the benefactor also comes into play. A deadpool has been created for all supernatural creatures of beacon hills; everyone is out for bloods, even non-hunters.

  • Season 5

    • Dread doctors have re-emerged and are out for blood. This mysterious goup of ancient scientist are taking humans and turning them into unatural supernatural creatures, most of them or dying in the process. The few that live through the transformation are out killing. Ghost riders are taking the citizens of Beacon Hills and erasing them from existing. One of the first characters taken is Stiles. Most of the season centers on how to get everyone back and remembering who they lost in the first place.

  • Season 6

    • Its the end of senior year and the gang is getting ready for their next adventures... Beacon Hills has different plans for them. Fear, a creature that can turn people to stone and incite madening fear, has emerged. Most of the town has gone crazy. Differnet gangs of amateur hunters have spung up, killing everything and everyone insight. The local police have turned on Sheriff Stilinski and persecuting supernatural creatures.
